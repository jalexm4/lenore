About Lenore

What is Lenore?

Lenore is a 2D sidescroller web game made for SET08101

What technology stack is used?

Pure vanilla HTML, CSS, and JS (Through TypeScript)

What is the point of the game?

The goal is to reach the end of the level and progress to the next one.

Is the game finished?

No, the game is nowhere near finished and will require a lot of time and effort to finish.
